Coto Makassar

Coto Makassar or Coto Mangkasara (Makassarese),
an Indonesian traditional soup originating from Makassar, South Sulawesi.
a variant of soto traditional beef and offal stew with seasoned broth made
from ground peanuts and spices.The main ingredient of this soup is beef
it can be mixed with innards such as intestine, liver, lungs, heart, tripe,
or cow brain.
Coto Makassar is usually served with Burasa or Ketupat rice cakes.
